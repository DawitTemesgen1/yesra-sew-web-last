## Access Instructions

1. **Launch the Yesra Sew app**

2. **Sign In:**
   - Tap "Sign In" button
   - Choose "Email" or "Phone" method
   - Enter credentials above
   - Tap "Login"

3. **Available Features:**
   - ✅ Browse all categories (Jobs, Homes, Cars, Tenders)
   - ✅ View listing details
   - ✅ Post new listings
   - ✅ Chat with sellers
   - ✅ Save favorites
   - ✅ Manage profile
   - ✅ Search and filter
   - ✅ Change language (English, Amharic, Oromo, Tigrinya)

4. **Testing Chat:**
   - Open any listing
   - Tap "Chat Now" button
   - Send a test message

5. **Testing Post Listing:**
